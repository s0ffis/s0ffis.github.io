Margherita Ferrari

I'm a teacher and edutainer with a background in social research. I'm passionate about finding ways to make tech education inclusive and fun.

I've worked at MUSE FabLab and I'm currently a tutor at Girls Code it Better. I'm based in Vicenza and available for workshops all over Italy.

My Focus

Edutainment for Kids

I design and offer hands-on workshops that foster the connection between technology, science and play.

Digital Fabrication and Open Source Technology

I believe in facilitating the use of open source technology for students of all ages.

Inclusivity in Tech

I work very hard on making my activities non-intimidating and empowering for all.

Effects Building 101

I'm currently putting together a touring summer workshop devoted to the building of effect pedals. It's going to be an introductory activity for folks interested in creating their own stompboxes, in a friendly and inclusive environment. The workshop is aimed at women, non-binary and queer folks.

If you're interested in organizing this workshop in your town, please get in touch.

Circuit Making Repositories

While I was working at MUSE FabLab, I documented the entirety of Circuit Making, an open source kit and didactic tool for teaching how electric circuits work.
